⬡ Tendon Health Protocol — applies every session
Slow eccentrics are your best friend. On all lower body exercises, take 3–4s on the lowering phase. This is the primary driver of tendon adaptation. No bouncing at the bottom of squats, lunges, or RDLs — control through full range. Warm up the knees first with 2 min light cycling or leg swings before any leg work. For patella specifically: Spanish squats and wall sits (isometric holds 30–45s) 2–3× per week are excellent prehab. Collagen + Vit C 30–60 min before training supports tendon synthesis.
